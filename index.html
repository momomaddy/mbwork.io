I’m not a traditional Brand, Marketing, or Comms hire.

I operate more like an in-house, integrated agency — plugged into the business, not orbiting it.

Across roles, I’ve built visibility, credibility, and revenue — selling forex cards., railway tickets, hearing aids, CNG, PNG, and cooking oil; while also launching ESG platforms, EV charging networks, and renewable energy businesses.

On paper, it looks scattered. In reality, it’s the same job repeated across contexts. My career has been a middle-class man’s grocery list — different items, same basket, same decisions.

From One Nation, Billion Celebrations to behaviour-change work with Police Scotland, I’ve built campaigns that don’t just get seen — they get acted on.

That approach comes from working like a newsroom, not a campaign factory. I’ve operated alongside founders and leadership teams when things were stable — and when they weren’t. From on-ground safety incidents to farmer protests, to being inside a $100B reputational crisis with global media watching every move.

Different situations. Same expectation: get it right when it counts.

The question I solve is always the same: How do we reach them, convert them, retain them — and build a system that does it repeatedly?

That’s the hire.

Not someone who runs campaigns, but someone who builds communication that holds — across growth, scale, and uncertainty.
